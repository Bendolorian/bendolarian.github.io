Overview

During the course of your apprenticeship you will build a portfolio of evidence. This portfolio of evidence such demonstrate your journey over the course of your apprenticeship. It should be rich in evidence; showcasing key elements and tasks that you do in your workplace. This portfolio will form part of Assessment method 1:An Hour long professional discussion with an independent assessor at the end of your apprenticeship In the below themes you will notice notes such as K1, B2, S3; these are the knowledge, skills and behaviours that you need to meet to pass the appreticeship.

1. Communication

1.1 Method

Checkpoint: Does your portfolio evidence the below? And can you confidently answer these key points?
  • How do you communciate with stakeholders?
  • Does your communciation differ for urgent, medium and low level tasks?
  • How does your communication style differ when dealing with non technical people?
  • How does your communication style cater for diversity and inclusion?
  • How do you escalate tasks? What is your role in the process? Do you follow the escalation and reflect?
